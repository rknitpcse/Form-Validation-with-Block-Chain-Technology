SCIS Students

The M.Tech programmes are designed to provide a strong foundation in the concepts of Computer Sciences . These programmes bridge the gap between core subjects and advanced topics, keeping pace with the industrial requirements. This course also exposes the students to the "happening fields" in the world of computer science. The grooming of students starts right from day one, through highly interactive treatment involving classroom discussions, seminars, lab assignments, mini projects and term papers. The students are molded in all facets, to face the challenges in the field. Companies and Institutions are involved in giving their feedback regarding the courses offered, which are under constant review, thus making the programme more relevant and compatible to the growing IT industry needs.
